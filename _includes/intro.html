Hi, my name is Xavier I am a mathematical physicist living in Brisbane, Australia. I am currently finalising my PhD at the University of Queensland. My main area of interest is the study of statistical mechanical models that give rise to quantum field theories in their continuum limit. Other research interest include quantum control and machine learning, at Max Kelsen we use the latter to attack the former two.
